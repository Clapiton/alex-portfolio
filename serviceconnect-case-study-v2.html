Problem

A Fragmented Service Economy

  • Customers struggled to find trustworthy, verified professionals for home and personal services — discovery was scattered across WhatsApp groups, referrals, and unreliable directories.
  • Skilled service providers had no unified platform to market themselves, manage bookings, track earnings, and communicate with clients professionally.
  • No existing platform offered both a premium customer experience AND a powerful provider-side management tool in a single cohesive product.
2

Solution

A Smart, Unified Service Ecosystem

  • Customer App — Designed for ease; browse verified providers, view ratings, book services, and pay securely — all in one flow.
  • Provider Dashboard — Enables professionals to manage incoming jobs, track real-time earnings, and monitor performance metrics.
  • AI Assistant (GPT-4o) — An embedded assistant that understands natural language requests, recommends the right services, and guides users through the platform.
  • Nearby Providers Map — Real-time geolocation showing available professionals within the user's proximity, with live status indicators.
  • Scalable Architecture — Firebase + Flutter stack built to support multi-city expansion, new service verticals, and future SaaS integrations.
3

Result

A Streamlined, High-Performance Marketplace

  • Customers can discover, compare, and book a service provider in under 3 minutes — eliminating the traditional back-and-forth of finding help.
  • Providers gain full visibility into their schedule and income, reducing missed bookings and improving professional reliability.
  • The AI assistant significantly reduces drop-off by guiding unsure users to the right service category instantly.
  • Stripe integration ensures secure, frictionless payment for both parties — building trust on both sides of the marketplace.
  • Single Flutter codebase delivers a consistent, premium experience across iOS, Android, and Web — maximizing reach with minimal overhead.
